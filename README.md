
# 📊 Vrinda Store Report 2022

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing sales performance, customer demographics, and order trends for Vrinda Store in 2022.

## 🖼️ Dashboard Preview

![Vrinda Store Report 2022](dashboard.png)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Vrinda Store Report 2022** dashboard provides insights into:

* Monthly sales performance
* Order trends
* Gender-based sales comparison
* Age group analysis
* Sales distribution by state
* Order status breakdown
* Sales by sales channel

This project helps stakeholders understand customer behavior and sales performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Orders vs Sales

* Tracks monthly revenue and total number of orders.
* Identifies seasonal trends and peak months.

### 2️⃣ Sales: Men vs Women

* Women contribute **64%** of total sales.
* Men contribute **36%** of total sales.

### 3️⃣ Order Status

* Majority of orders are **Delivered**.
* Small percentage marked as **Cancelled**, **Returned**, or **Refunded**.

### 4️⃣ Top 5 States by Sales

* Maharashtra – Highest sales
* Karnataka
* Uttar Pradesh
* Telangana
* Tamil Nadu

### 5️⃣ Orders by Age & Gender

* Adult women contribute the highest share.
* Teenager and senior categories show moderate engagement.

### 6️⃣ Orders by Sales Channel

Sales channels analyzed:

* Ajio
* Amazon
* Flipkart
* Meesho
* Myntra
* Nalli
* Others

Amazon and Flipkart contribute significantly to total orders.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the Excel file.
2. Open in Microsoft Excel (2016 or later recommended).
3. Use slicers to filter by month, channel, or category.
4. Analyze charts for insights.

## Conclusion

The Vrinda Store Report 2022 shows that the business performed well throughout the year. Women customers contributed the most to sales, especially in the adult age group. Maharashtra and Karnataka were the top-performing states, and Amazon and Flipkart were the leading sales channels.

Overall, the store has strong sales performance with most orders successfully delivered, indicating good business growth and customer demand.
